Swarm: Movement For Bodies and Strings


Swarm: Collaborative Instrument and Installation with Josh Ezra Breen Tucci (2019)

Swarm is an audio-visual instrument consisting of a series of 8 stringed instruments that evokes the droning sounds of a metallic swarm. The instruments are tuned at different dissonant intervals and are controlled by a distance sensor and Arduino. The position of the body is tracked in space to correspond to the position of the real-time generative visuals.
