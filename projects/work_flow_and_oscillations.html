Flow and Oscillations

Year: 2024
Description: Performance and installation with Zhen Zhen Zhong as part of The Residency Project, Pasadena, CA. This project explores the interplay between water, movement, and sound, using live performance and generative visuals. 8.1 channel Sound and Media Installation. 8 surface transducers, 1 bass shaker, projection mapping, tubes, bubbles, and motors.
