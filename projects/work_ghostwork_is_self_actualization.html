Ghostwork Is Self Actualization

Year: 2025
Ghostwork is Self Actualization is a solo multimedia performance piece that explores how automatic captioning and dictation systems reshapes human language. Moving through four distinct phases, the piece traces a path from data acquisition, to digitization of speech, the cloning of speech, and to its complete disintegration into sound fragments. Using four-channel audio, computer monitors, and video projections, the artist explores how systems reduce language into data, separating it from contextual meaning, the spoken voice, and identity. Furthermore, these broken fragments of words and computerized speech are then able to be rearranged and subsequently dictated and randomized as an infinite feedback system.
