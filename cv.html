Visiting Lecturer – Music and Artificial Intelligence

Fall 2024 - Occidental College, Los Angeles, CA

Designed and taught course on machine learning, generative AI, and music. Lead discussions on contemporary topics related to AI and cultural production, labor, music industry, ethics, copyright, and socio-political issues.

Taught technical workshops about fine-tuning recurrent neural networks with custom datasets, MIDI generation, computer vision, text to audio for sound fx, neural synthesis using Python.
